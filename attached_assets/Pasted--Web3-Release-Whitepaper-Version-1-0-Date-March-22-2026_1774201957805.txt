# Web3 Release Whitepaper

**Version: 1.0**  
**Date: March 22, 2026**  
**Project Name: Web3 Release**  
**Positioning: One-Stop Demand Publishing and Matching Platform for Web3 Crypto Teams**

## 1. Abstract

Web3 Release is a decentralized, community-driven Web3 collaboration platform designed to connect **crypto teams**, **KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders)**, and **developers**, building a transparent and efficient hub for blockchain ecosystem publishing and collaboration.

The platform enables real identity verification through wallet connections, allowing seamless interactions for crypto team needs, talent matching, knowledge sharing, and community engagement. Its core goal is to address long-standing issues in the Web3 ecosystem—fragmentation, lack of trust, and insufficient incentives—accelerating healthier and faster real-world adoption of blockchain innovation.

## 2. Current Pain Points in the Web3 Ecosystem

Despite rapid growth in the blockchain industry as of 2026, the following core challenges persist:

- Crypto teams struggle to efficiently find reliable developers, security auditors, node operators, or community partners. Information is scattered across Twitter, Discord, Telegram, and other channels, resulting in extremely high screening costs.
- KOLs frequently receive numerous low-quality collaboration requests, making it difficult to assess project legitimacy, while high-quality content creators lack sustained incentive mechanisms.
- Developers face unequal opportunities in on-chain projects, remaining invisible and struggling to match with truly valuable tasks (e.g., bug bounties, hackathons, node recruitment).
- The overall ecosystem suffers from information asymmetry, trust barriers, redundant development, low-quality collaborations, rampant scams, and reduced efficiency in genuine innovation.

## 3. Core Solutions Provided by Web3 Release

Web3 Release offers a unified, permissionless collaboration space where all interactions are based on wallet addresses, ensuring pseudonymity with traceability. The main user roles fall into three categories:

### 3.1 Crypto Teams
- Can publish various needs and announcements, including but not limited to:
  - Testnet recruitment
  - IDO/Launchpad collaborations
  - Security audit requests
  - Integration announcements
  - Airdrop plans
  - Funding/recruitment/node recruitment
  - Hackathons/bug bounties
- All published content appears in the homepage "Project Showcase" area and corresponding dedicated sections in a timeline format, with the latest content prioritized and pinned at the top.
- First-time publications automatically receive a standard homepage display slot. Subsequent updates refresh the card's activity and ranking (each crypto team is limited to one homepage card slot to ensure diversity).

### 3.2 KOLs (Key Opinion Leaders)
- Can publish collaboration intentions, project reviews, market insights, AMAs, and more.
- Dedicated KOL section + KOL leaderboard, automatically ranked based on real engagement metrics such as likes, comments, and views.
- The top 200 most active KOLs each month receive ecosystem airdrop rewards, creating a positive incentive loop.

### 3.3 Developers
- Can publish technical shares, job intentions, open-source contributions, bug bounty tasks, etc.
- Dedicated "Developers" section where all developer posts are vertically displayed, making it easy for crypto teams to quickly screen talent.
- Developer content does not occupy standard homepage display slots, focusing instead on professional domain exposure.

## 4. Points System (Core Incentive Mechanism)

Web3 Release features a built-in points system to incentivize high-quality content creation and community participation. The total points supply is unlimited and can be used for future airdrop redemptions or other ecosystem benefits.

**General User Points Sources**:
- Daily check-in: +1,000 points
- Likes: +100 points per like (daily cap applies)
- Comments: +100 points per comment (first 10 valid, daily cap applies)
- Referral rewards: 20% of the referred user's daily points automatically allocated to the referrer (no cap)

**KOL-Exclusive Points Sources** (KOLs do not participate in general check-in/like/comment points):
- +10 points per like on their post
- +10 points per comment on their post
- +50 points per 1,000 views on their post
- Referral rewards same as above

Points reflect genuine influence and contribution levels, serving as a key weighting factor for future airdrop allocations.

## 5. Contributions to the Blockchain Field and Problems Solved

Web3 Release delivers tangible value to the blockchain ecosystem in the following ways:

1. **Significantly Reduces Collaboration Friction**  
   Crypto team demands and developer/KOL supply are matched in real time on a single platform, minimizing cross-platform searches and trust costs.

2. **Establishes a Verifiable Reputation System**  
   Based on wallet addresses + engagement data (likes, comments, views), it forms a decentralized reputation system that reduces scam risks.

3. **Incentivizes Genuine Contributions Over Traffic Cheating**  
   Points are earned from real interactions, enabling KOLs and developers to receive ongoing rewards through high-quality content and fostering a healthy content ecosystem.

4. **Promotes Multi-Chain Collaboration and Information Flow**  
   Unified display of cross-chain information such as testnets, airdrops, audits, and hackathons accelerates ecosystem interconnectivity.

5. **Empowers Developers and Mid-Tier KOLs**  
   Provides zero-barrier exposure channels, allowing truly capable individuals to be discovered more easily without relying on centralized platform algorithms or paid promotions.

## 6. Vision

Web3 Release aims to become the "Twitter + LinkedIn + Gitcoin" of the Web3 era—a truly decentralized, community self-driven collaboration and publishing hub.

We believe that when crypto teams, KOLs, and developers can freely publish and collaborate in an environment of trust, traceability, and sufficient incentives, the next wave of genuine blockchain innovation will accelerate.
